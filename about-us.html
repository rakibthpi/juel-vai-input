ABOUT US

Wellbeing is a healthcare service provider dedicated to ensuring quality medical care and health services for Bangladeshi expatriates and their families worldwide. We understand the healthcare challenges faced by expatriates and offer free telemedicine, health insurance, mental health support, and emergency medical assistance. We also ensure healthcare services for their families in their absence. Our goal is to provide affordable, accessible, safe, and reliable healthcare so that Bangladeshi expatriates can ensure their and their families' health security even while staying abroad.
